modular_nova/modules/protean/readme.md

MODULE ID: PROTEAN

Description

Adds the Protean species - a nanomachine-based lifeform that sustains itself by consuming metal. Proteans are immortal; when critically damaged they retreat into their integrated modsuit core. They can assimilate other modsuits, lock their suit onto other players, and toggle low power mode.

TG Proc Changes

None - fully modular.

Defines

  • SPECIES_PROTEAN - Species identifier
  • ORGAN_NANOMACHINE - Organ flag for nanomachine organs (bit 19)
  • Various protean-specific constants in code/_defines.dm
