Kinetic Crusher Conversion Kit

Lavaland's "Hard Mode" option for players, requiring melee attacks (backstabs even better), but allowing you to upgrade it with trophies gained from fighting lavaland monsters, making it a good tradeoff and a decent playstyle. This item overhauls all the crushers, merging them into one item which allows them to choose one of the variants.
