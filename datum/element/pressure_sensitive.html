pressure_sensitive
/datum/element/pressure_sensitive

When attached to a living mob, causes it to take damage over time when pressure is too low or too high as defined by the element's arguments.

Vars

high_pressure_damageThe damage taken when pressure is above max_pressure. 0 or below disables high pressure damage.
low_pressure_damageThe damage taken when pressure is below min_pressure. 0 or below disables low pressure damage.
max_pressureThe pressure (in kilopascals) above which high_pressure_damage is taken.
min_pressureThe pressure (in kilopascals) below which low_pressure_damage is taken.
